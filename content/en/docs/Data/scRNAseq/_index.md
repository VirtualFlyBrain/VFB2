Virtual Fly Brain incorporates scRNAseq data from several datasets. This data is taken from FlyBase, which in turn takes it from the EBI Single Cell Expression Atlas.

## Notable sRNAseq Datasets Hosted by VFB

- Fly Cell Atlas
- Aging Fly Cell Atlas
